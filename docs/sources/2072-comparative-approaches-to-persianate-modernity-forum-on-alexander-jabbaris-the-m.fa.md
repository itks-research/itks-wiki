## چکیده

This forum brings together scholars of Persianate studies to accomplish two goals: (1) introduce CLS readers to new scholarship on Persianate modernity; (2) initiate a comparative exchange among scholars of the Middle East on questions of comparative methodology, (post)secularism, literary history and literary modernity, poetics and sexuality
