## چکیده

In 2020, the Lancet Commission introduced a meta‐analysis featuring a life course model encompassing 12 potentially modifiable risk factors for dementia: low education, hypertension, hearing impairment, smoking, obesity, depression, physical inactivity, diabetes, low social contact, alcohol consumption, traumatic brain injury, and air pollution. The aim of this study is to investigate the association of the aforementioned risk factors with the incidence of dementia with the incidence of dementia in the Czech Republic, capitalizing on a nation‐wide population‐based study.
