## چکیده

It has often been argued that oil, gas and minerals may have a negative impact on development as measured by&#13;\nincome per capita. This does not say much about sustainability, which is critical for developing countries whose&#13;\neconomic growth derives primarily from the exploitation of exhaustible resources.
