## Abstract

ABSTRACT This study reports the draft genome sequence of Pseudomonas sp. MAHUQ-62, a bacterial strain isolated from the soil sample of a pumpkin garden located in Anseong, South Korea, in 2020. The genome consists of 6,038,011 base pairs assembled into 57 contigs, encoding 5,478 predicted protein-coding genes.
