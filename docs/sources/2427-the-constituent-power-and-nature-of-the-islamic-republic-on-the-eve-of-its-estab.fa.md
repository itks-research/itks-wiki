## چکیده

The first principle of the constitution declares the government of Iran to be the Republic, and its basis is the will of the nation (founding power), which was expressed in the referendum of April 1979. The process of establishment and legal establishment of the Islamic Republic of Iran has gone through three stages of the Islamic Republic as a political by direct popular vote, establishing the structure of the Islamic Republic in the constitution approved by the people's representatives, and finalizing the constitution by direct popular vote. The sources and texts of the first category of libraries and with a comparative method, focus on discovering the purpose of the founding power up to the first stage of the founding level, ie determining the Islamic Republic as a political regime As a manifestation of the supreme will of the nation (founding power) - to investigate in the period leading up to the referendum to determine the system. Research findings have shown that republic in the promised system of the Islamic Republic is not compatible with any of the common types of republics today, including the so-called - democratic republics, and the term Islamism of this system is the essential element of government (in terms of The adherence of the head of state to the law) differs from democratic republics in a different way.
