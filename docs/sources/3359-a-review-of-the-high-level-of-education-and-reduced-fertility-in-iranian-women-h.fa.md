## چکیده

In 1994, female empowerment and gender equality were emphasized as global priorities at the Fourth International Conference on Population and Development (1). Since then, extensive effort has been made to extend female empowerment internationally (2). Today, empowering women is a controversial issue in development (3). It is considered as the main indicator of women's status (4).
