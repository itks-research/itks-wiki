## چکیده

ABSTRACT Vibrio harveyi strain 22FBVib0145 was isolated from a diseased olive flounder farmed in Jeju, Korea. Here, we report the draft genome sequence of this strain. It is 6,238,277 bp in length with a G + C content of 44.8%.
