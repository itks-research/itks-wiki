## Abstract

&quot;Lustration&quot; is a relatively new term in Ukrainian political vocabulary. Ukrainian researchers are talking about &quot;state&quot;, &quot;economic&quot; and &quot;political&quot; types of lustration. State lustration is aimed at the defense of the state institutions against the secret influence of the interested persons, the special services, secret organizations or foreign influence. Economic lustration supposed to prevent the influence of oligarchs and shadow capital on the national economy. Mass falsifications of the results of the presidential elections in 2004, violations of human rights, censorship in mass media, and persecution of the opposition were the main reasons that led to the start of the discussion about lustration in Ukraine. There are many interpretations of the meaning of lustration, its methods and terms. Among Ukrainian lawyers, the dominant point of view is that lustration is a more political, not judicial phenomenon. There are many discussions that total lustration is impossible in Ukraine, as well as the possibility of transformation into a &quot;witch hunt&quot;.
