Click to increase image sizeClick to decrease image size Additional informationNotes on contributorsRuth Greenspan BellRuth Greenspan Bell, director of institutional development and environmental assistance at Resources for the Future in Washington, D.C., works with countries and NGOs to build more effective systems of environmental protection. She has lived in Poland and worked there and elsewhere in the former Soviet bloc on environmental assistance and cooperation efforts. She has also worked as senior advisor to the assistant secretary of state for oceans and international environmental and scientific affairs and in various management positions in the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency's Office of General Counsel. She has published extensively about ways to stimulate better implementation of domestic and international environmental requirements in the developing world and the countries in transition. She can be contacted at Bell@rff.org.
