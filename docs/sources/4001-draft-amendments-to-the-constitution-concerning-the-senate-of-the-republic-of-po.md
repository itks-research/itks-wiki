## Abstract

The article discusses successive draft laws amending the Constitution in the area concerning the second chamber of parliament (the Senate). The timeframe covers the years 1997–2025, from the entry into force of the Constitution to the proposals submitted in the current term. Reference is also made to doctrinal proposals. The analysis reveals a lack of coherence in the vision of changes in the examined area.
