## چکیده

ABSTRACT The draft genome of Solimonas sp. strain SE-A11 bacterium isolated from the rhizosphere of feral Brassica napus in South Korea, represents the first isolate of the genus Solimonas obtained from a plant rhizosphere. The strain has a genome size of 4,937,440  bp, a G + C content of 66.5%, and 281 predicted subsystems.
