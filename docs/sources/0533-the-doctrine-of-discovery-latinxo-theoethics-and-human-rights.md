## Abstract

Introduction 1992 marked the quincentenary of the violent clash between Spanish Europeans and Indigenous and originary peoples of what we know today as the Americas.In 2015 the Indian Residential Schools Truth and Reconciliation Commission (TRC) published the report of the long-standing history of human rights abuses in the Canadian residential school system.The Canadian report is the most recent documented record of the long history of violations of human rights in the Americas following a long list of reports from similar commissions in Latin America including Argentina
