## چکیده

ABSTRACT This study reports the draft genome sequence of Cohnella sp. M.A.Huq-80, a bacterial strain isolated from the soil sample of a pond located in Anseong, South Korea, in 2021. The genome consists of 6,429,982 base pairs assembled into 45 contigs, encoding 5,416 predicted protein coding genes. Members of the genus Cohnella are significant for their roles in soil ecology and for producing thermostable enzymes valuable in biotechnology and biomass degradation.
