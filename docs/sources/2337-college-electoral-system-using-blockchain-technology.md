## Abstract

Elections play a significant role in democratic systems, extending beyond governmental administrations to educational institutions such as colleges. Within colleges, students act as voters and participate in elections to choose their union leaders. However, traditional electoral systems in colleges often encounter challenges related to transparency, security, and accountability. Fortunately, blockchain technology has emerged as a potential solution to enhance various domains, including voting systems. In this project, we propose a novel approach that harnesses the benefits of blockchain technology to address these challenges. Our objective is to design and implement a blockchain-based electoral system specifically tailored for college environment. The proposed system aims to enhance the authenticity of voters, improve the security of the system, and ensure the integrity and efficiency of the voting process while safeguarding the privacy of individual voters. Through the implementation of this College Electoral System using Blockchain, we aspire to improve the voting experience in colleges, fostering a fair, secure, and transparent democratic process.
