## Abstract

Socioeconomic factors, through the mechanism of psychosocial determinants, may significantly affect women's empowerment in making decisions regarding reproductive health. Conditions associated with these factors should be improved to ensure that women claim and exercise their right to have mastery over their reproductive health.
