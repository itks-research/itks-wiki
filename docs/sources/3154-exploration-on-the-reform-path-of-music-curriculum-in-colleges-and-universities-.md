## Abstract

The new media era has generated the need for reform in music education at higher education institutions. This research constructs a new music teaching technology architecture, developing an integrated teaching platform that combines real-time audio analysis, music feature extraction, intelligent theory teaching, and performance posture recognition. Through system architecture design and module development, core algorithms and technological breakthroughs are achieved, establishing a complete teaching ecosystem. Educational experiments prove that this system significantly improves learning efficiency and quality, with notable enhancement in students' music comprehension abilities, performance skills, and creative levels. The research provides practical references for the digital transformation of music courses in higher education institutions and promotes innovation in education and teaching models.
