## چکیده

The protection of monuments and broadly understood cultural heritage is one of the important tasks that the Constitution of the Republic of Poland places on its authorities. In order to implement this constitutional task, the Act on the protection of monuments and the care of monuments provides for special powers for public administration bodies to suspend construction works that apply to both monuments and objects that can potentially become such monuments. When drafting regulations in this area, the legislator did not avoid a few mistakes and was imprecise in several places, which may result in significant practical problems.
