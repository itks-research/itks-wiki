## چکیده

The meeting marks the start of a new phase of the MENA-OECD Initiative on Governance and Competitiveness for Development, a platform for policy dialogue at both political and technical levels.The report aims to contribute to the substantive discussions and decisions at the Ministerial Conference and to the future activities of MENA-OECD initiatives.Together, the OECD and its MENA partners will continue to design, develop and deliver better policies for better lives across the MENA region.
