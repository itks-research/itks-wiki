## چکیده

Europeisation of public administration may be equally a subject of an analysis in the course of&#13;\nadministrative law or administration as well as management. A characteristic feature of the recent&#13;\nyears, however, has been a theoretical departing of the two approaches, leading to major practical&#13;\nconsequences. The paper discusses the main normative, structural and functional aspects of the&#13;\nEuropeisation of administration at the national and Community level. It also shows the main&#13;\n(fundamental) legal and administrative consequences of Europeisation of administration in Poland,&#13;\nand the effects o f the gradual development of common European administration. Special attention has&#13;\nbeen given the theoretical clash of the so-called classical community method with the idea of new&#13;\ngovernance and its main form existing in the Community - an open coordination method.&#13;\nWhat is particularly interesting is that the functioning o f the rapidly developing network&#13;\nadministration and the growing number o f EU agencies, all have relatively feeble legal grounds in the&#13;\ntreaty, a fact that has been criticised since long ago. Moreover, contrary to the expectations, neither&#13;\nthe draft of the European Constitution, nor the Reforming Treaty, have changed much in that respect.
