## Abstract

ABSTRACT This report outlines the draft genome sequences of six Klebsiella spp. strains from South Korea’s agricultural produce and environments. Genome sizes ranged from 5.25 to 6.21 Mbp with 55.64% to 57.55% GC content. Each strain contained multiple plasmid sequences identified by PlasmidFinder, indicating significant antimicrobial resistance.
