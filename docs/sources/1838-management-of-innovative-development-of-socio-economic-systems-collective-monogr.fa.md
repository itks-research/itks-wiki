## چکیده

The collective monograph is dedicated to the study of the current state of management of the innovative development of socio-economic systems. The authors focused attention on modern aspects of development, researching the potential of socio-economic systems at the macro-, meso- and micro-level, building a strategy and modeling the innovative development of socio-economic systems. Financial and personnel support for the innovative development of socio-economic systems was separately investigated.
